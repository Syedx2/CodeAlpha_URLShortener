Privacy Policy

At Sniplink, your privacy is our top priority. We believe in simplicity and utility without invasive data harvesting. This Privacy Policy details how we handle the minimal data generated when you use our service.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike traditional link shorteners or analytics platforms, Sniplink does not collect or store:

  • Your IP address or geographic location
  • Browser user-agent, device details, or operating system characteristics
  • Personal identifying information (PII) such as your name, phone number, or physical address
  • Tracking histories across third-party websites

2. Information We Do Collect

To perform the core URL redirection service and display statistics, our backend database stores:

  • The Original URL you request to shorten.
  • The generated Short Code linked to that original URL.
  • The **Creation Date & Time** of the shortened link.
  • An aggregate **Click Counter** that increments each time the redirect link is accessed.

3. Cookies and LocalStorage

We do **not** use tracking, analytics, advertising, or profiling cookies on our server.

To allow you to keep track of the URLs you have shortened, we utilize standard HTML5 **LocalStorage** directly inside your web browser. This feature saves your recent link records strictly on your physical device. It is never uploaded, shared with, or accessible by our servers or any third parties. You can clear this history at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

4. Security

We employ standard data protection methods to safeguard the link configurations stored in our database. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Links

Our service redirects users to external web links created by other individuals. Sniplink is not responsible for the privacy practices, cookie policies, or content of those destination websites. We highly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to the developer at syeds.devs@gmail.com.
